Mid-Side

Description: One Cardioid (Mid) and one figure-8 (Side) microphone, with the Side mic capturing left/right differences.

  • Impossible to record an uneven stereo image
  • The 'Mid' and the 'Side' channels can be treated with different equalisation and compression.
    • Solidify the centre.
    • Add sparkle to the stereo.
  • The balance between the mono central image and the side/stereo width can be adjusted after recording.

Tips for Competency:
✔ Offers adjustable stereo width in post-production.
✔ Requires decoding (sum and difference processing) for stereo playback.
✔ Excellent for broadcast, film, and mono-compatible recordings.
✔ Works well in unpredictable environments due to post-mix flexibility.
