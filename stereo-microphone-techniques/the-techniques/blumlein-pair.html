Blumlein Pair

Description: Two figure-8 microphones at a 90° angle, coincident placement.

Tips for Competency:
✔ Captures a highly immersive stereo image with natural depth.
✔ Works well in well-treated or acoustically pleasing rooms.
✔ Be mindful of sound reflections from the back lobes of the mics.
✔ Ideal for solo instruments, duets, and small ensembles.
