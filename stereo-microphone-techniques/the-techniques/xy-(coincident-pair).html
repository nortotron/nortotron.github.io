Tips for Competency

Description: Two directional microphones (usually cardioid) placed at a 90° angle, capsules as close together as possible.

Tips for Competency:
✔ Ensure capsules are as close as possible to avoid phase issues.
✔ Best for mono compatibility and capturing a balanced stereo image.
✔ Works well for small ensembles and close-miked sources.
✔ Avoid excessive spacing between mics—use a stereo bar for precision.
